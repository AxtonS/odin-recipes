The Most Supreme Pizza!

Description:

All that stands between you and the best version of a pizza is 30 minutes and a pre-baked crust.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1(12 ounce) bag Recipe Ready Pizza Supreme Blend
  • 1(10 ounce) pre-baked whole wheat or regular pizza crust
  • 1 cup prepared pizza sauce
  • 1 ounce slided pepperoni
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.
  2. Heat oil in large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat and cook Recipe Ready Pizza Supreme Blend, stirring occasionally, 10 minutes or until vegetables are softened and any liquid is absorbed.
  3. Arrange pizza crust on baking sheet and evenly spread with pizza sauce. Top with vegetable mixture and pepperoni. Sprinkle with cheese. Bake 10 minutes or until cheese is melted.
